Online Voting System

The aim of electronic voting schemes is to provide a set of protocols that allow voters to cast ballots while a group of authorities collect votes and output the final tally. Problems with voting machines extend from the quality of the locks, to the need for a printed audit trail, to the hacking of the communication links.

This system has been developed to simplify the process of organizing elections and make it convenient for voters to vote remotely from their home computers while taking into consideration security, anonymity and providing auditioning capabilities.Online Voting System provides the online registration form for the users before voting and makes the users to cast their vote online. The system is to be developed user friendly.
